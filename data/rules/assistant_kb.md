# TN DCR (TNCDBR-2019) — Assistant Knowledge Base

Curated rules most relevant to design-stage doubts (setback encroachments, projections,
staircases, balconies, FSI exclusions). Authoritative source: Tamil Nadu Combined
Development & Building Rules 2019 + amendments (notably GO 69/2024 raising Non-High-Rise
height limit 12m → 14m). Answer ONLY from these; if a query is outside them, say so.

## Definitions (Rule 2)
- **Balcony (2-13):** a horizontal projection, cantilevered or otherwise, to serve as a passage or sitting-out place.
- **Canopy/portico/porch (2-18):** a cantilever projection at lintel or ground-floor-roof level over an entrance.
- **Corridor (2-26):** a common passage or circulation space within a building.
- **Verandah (2-123):** a covered area with at least one side open to the outside.

## Rule 28 — Structures permitted IN the minimum setback spaces
Unless specifically provided elsewhere, NO structure may be built within the minimum prescribed setback, EXCEPT:
**(a) Non-High-Rise buildings:**
- (i) Sun-shades not exceeding **0.6 m** projection.
- (ii) Motor room ≤ 2 sq.m each, height ≤ 1.8 m, not affecting parking/driveway.
- (iii) For NHR up to **9.0 m height**: open single-flight / spiral / open double-flight **staircase**, provided it is **not within 0.50 m of the side boundary, nor within 1 m of the front/rear boundary** (or street alignment).
- (iv) Compound wall height ≤ 2.0 m.
- (v) Watchman booth ≤ 2.5 m × 2.5 m, height ≤ 3 m, at each gate.
- (vi) Gate pillars (with/without arches), min headroom 5.50 m over ≥ 3.5 m width.
- (vii) Meter rooms / electrical panels along boundary/external walls, projection ≤ 0.60 m, plus open transformer (not affecting parking/driveway), per TNEB safety.
- Vehicular gates: max 2 (one entry + one exit); +1 gate per 50 m frontage beyond 50 m; +1 more if site abuts more than one road.

**Important:** Habitable spaces (rooms, toilets, kitchens, enclosed staircases for buildings >9 m) are NOT permitted in the setback. A toilet/room projecting into the setback is NOT allowed under Rule 28. Only the specific items above are permitted.

## Rule 29 — Spaces excluded from FSI & coverage
Not counted toward FSI/coverage: terrace stair/lift rooms & passages, architectural features, elevated tanks, WC ≤10 sq.m on terrace; stair/lift rooms & passages in stilt/basement parking floors; basement used for parking; open stilt parking floor; servant/driver WC per block; watchman & caretaker booths.

## Setbacks (Rule 35 Non-High-Rise — current, GO 69/2024)
- Applies to buildings up to **14 m** height (was 12 m).
- **Front setback by abutting road width:** ≤9 m → 1.5 m; >9–18 m → 3.0 m; >18–30.5 m → 4.5 m; >30.5 m → 6.0 m.
- **Side setback** (≤16 dwellings, by height & plot width): height ≤7 m → 1 m (plot ≤9 m one side, else either side); height >7–14 m → 1–1.5 m depending on plot width.
- **Rear setback:** height ≤7 m → Nil; >7–14 m → 1.5 m.
- Public buildings (kalyana mandapam, halls, hospitals, hotels, theatres, malls): setback all around ≥ 6 m.

## Setbacks (Rule 39 High-Rise, >18.30 m)
- Needs road ≥ 12 m (FSI 2.0), 15 m (2.5), 18 m (3.25). Max coverage 50%.
- Setback all around: 7 m up to 30 m height; +1 m per 6 m above 30 m, max 20 m.

## Rule 42 — Corridor / verandah width (cross-reference)
Minimum corridor/verandah widths are prescribed in Rule 42; balconies/corridors must not reduce required setbacks or open space.

## Guidance for answering
- A continuous balcony / cantilevered balcony may project as a horizontal projection but must respect the rules on projections into setbacks; balconies are generally measured into FSI unless specifically excluded; they cannot occupy the minimum required setback as a covered/enclosed space.
- "Can X project into the setback?" → check Rule 28's permitted list. If X (e.g., a toilet, a room, an enclosed staircase >9 m building) is not in the list, it is NOT permitted; suggest compliant alternatives (e.g., sun-shade ≤0.6 m, or keep the structure behind the setback line).
